public-benefit or scientific-impact deployments.

## What Counts as Commercial Use

Commercial use includes, but is not limited to:

- providing a paid product or service that uses EWCL model weights (including SaaS / API)
- integrating model weights into a commercial product, pipeline, or workflow
- internal use for commercial R&D at a for-profit entity
- selling, sublicensing, or distributing model weights or derivatives for value
